A hero network offers a visual representation of everything connected to a given individual or event within the project's knowledge graph. The hero (orange) sits at the center and radiating around them are all the nodes — events, sources, kin, enslavers, places — connected to them, and one another, by a web of intersecting relationships. Hover over a line or "edge" connecting two nodes to see the relationship between them.
